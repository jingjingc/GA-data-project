Global Musical Tweets

Geo-spatial clustering of micro-blogged music listening behavior

View project on GitHub

Introduction

This is the project page for a General Assembly Data Science final project involving the exploration of musical listening behavior as derived from Twitter social sharing activity.

Background

The motivation for this work stems from a desire to better understand the landscape of music and listening behavior from a spatial perspective.

Data

The primary source of data for this work is the Million Musical Tweets Dataset, which comprises over a million samples of tweets.
